About HeLx

HeLx is operated out of the Renaissance Computing Institute (RENCI)

HeLx was developed across the timeline of Renaissance Computing Institute (RENCI) at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill empowering diverse science communities to use advanced computing infrastructures.

Streamlined Data + Research

We learned that research communities have a wide array of data science tools in their daily toolbox, but provisioning these tools in modern cloud native environments with appropriate security, networking, and persistence support is daunting. HeLx serves as an on-ramp for these communities to assemble the right tools and to help them scale from on-premise to cloud infrastructures.
